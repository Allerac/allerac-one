# Allerac One — Product Value

> Reference document for landing page copy, marketing materials, and onboarding.
> Focus: what the user *gains*, not what the product *does*.

---

## The core promise

Most AI assistants are strangers. Every conversation starts from zero. They don't know your name, your job, what you've already asked, or what matters to you. You explain yourself over and over.

**Allerac One is an AI that actually knows you** — and runs entirely on your own infrastructure. Your data never leaves your control.

---

## Feature by feature: what you gain

---

### About me
*"The AI that remembers who you are."*

You tell Allerac your name, your profession, how you like to communicate, what you use it for. Once. From that moment on, every conversation starts already knowing you.

A doctor gets medical depth. A developer gets technical precision. A student gets patient explanations. You never have to say "I'm a software engineer, so..." again.

**What you gain:** An assistant calibrated to you, not a generic user.

---

### My Documents
*"Your personal knowledge base."*

Upload your own documents — contracts, research papers, manuals, notes, reports. Allerac reads them and can answer questions about them instantly, in plain language.

Ask "what are the payment terms in this contract?" or "summarize the key findings from this study." No need to read through 50 pages yourself.

**What you gain:** Your documents become searchable, queryable knowledge — available in seconds, in any conversation.

---

### Memories
*"The AI that remembers your conversations."*

Allerac automatically summarizes your past conversations and carries that context forward. If you discussed a project last week, it still knows about it today.

This is the difference between a tool you use and an assistant you *work with*. It builds a picture of your work, your interests, your ongoing projects — over time.

**What you gain:** Continuity. Every new conversation picks up where the last one left off.

---

### Skills
*"Different modes for different needs."*

Switch between specialized modes with one click. A web research assistant. A coding assistant. A health coach. Each skill has a focused purpose and the right tools activated.

You don't need to know about prompts or configurations. Just pick the mode that fits what you're about to do.

**What you gain:** Instant access to purpose-built assistants, without any setup.

---

### Web search
*"Real answers, not outdated ones."*

Allerac searches the web in real time when it needs to — weather, news, prices, current events. It doesn't guess or fabricate. It finds out.

Ask "what's the weather in Lisbon today?" or "what happened in the markets this morning?" and get an answer based on what's actually happening now.

**What you gain:** An assistant grounded in reality, not just training data from the past.

---

### Location awareness
*"The AI that knows where you are."*

Tell Allerac your city once. It uses that context automatically — for weather, for local recommendations, for anything that depends on where you are.

No need to specify your location every time you ask about something nearby.

**What you gain:** Answers relevant to your world, not a generic global one.

---

### Health dashboard
*"Your health data, finally in one place you can talk to."*

Connect your Garmin device and ask questions about your own health data in plain language. "How was my sleep this week?" "Am I walking enough?" "How is my body battery trending?"

No dashboards to interpret. No charts to decode. Just ask.

**What you gain:** Your health data becomes a conversation, not a spreadsheet.

---

### Telegram bot
*"Allerac, wherever you are."*

Access your personal AI from Telegram — on your phone, in any context, without opening a browser. Same memory, same knowledge, same skills.

Send a quick question from the subway. Get a summary while you're cooking. It's the same assistant, always available.

**What you gain:** Your AI in your pocket, not just on your desk.

---

### Scheduled tasks
*"The AI that works while you sleep."*

Set up automated prompts that run on a schedule and deliver results to your channels. A daily news briefing. A weekly health summary. A reminder with context.

You define what, when, and where. Allerac does the rest.

**What you gain:** Proactive intelligence — information delivered to you, without you having to ask.

---

### Privacy & self-hosting
*"Your AI. Your data. Your rules."*

Allerac One runs on your own server. Your conversations, your documents, your health data — none of it goes to a third party. No training on your data. No data sold. No surveillance.

You own the infrastructure. You own the data. You own the experience.

**What you gain:** The full power of a modern AI assistant, without giving up control of your private life.

---

### Local AI models (Ollama)
*"Works offline. No API costs. No limits."*

Run AI models locally on your own hardware. No internet required, no per-message costs, no rate limits. Great for sensitive data, air-gapped environments, or simply keeping costs at zero.

**What you gain:** Full AI capability without cloud dependency or ongoing fees.

## Who this is for

- **Professionals** who want an assistant that understands their field and their work
- **Researchers** who need to query large volumes of documents
- **Privacy-conscious users** who won't trade their data for convenience
- **Tech-savvy individuals** who want control over their AI setup
- **Anyone** tired of explaining themselves to a machine every single day
